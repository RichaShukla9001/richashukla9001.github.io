pangram, or holoalphabetic sentence, includes every letter of the alphabet at least once. The most challenging pangrams are the ones with the fewest letters. Here are a few of the best.:

The five boxing wizards jump quickly.Five quacking zephyrs jolt my wax bed.The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.Jinxed wizards pluck ivy from the big quilt.Crazy Fredrick bought many very exquisite opal jewels.We promptly judged antique ivory buckles for the next prize.A mad boxer shot a quick, gloved jab to the jaw of his dizzy opponent.Jaded zombies acted quaintly but kept driving their oxen forward.The job requires extra pluck and zeal from every young wage earner.
